# **App Name**: TRIXIRT

## Core Features:

- Sector Portfolio Display: Dedicated sections or pages for 'Neodymium Magnets,' 'Signages,' and 'Hydroponic Farming,' showcasing products, services, and expertise for each.
- About Us & Vision: A page detailing TRIXIRT's company history, mission, values, and overall vision.
- Project & Product Gallery: A visually engaging gallery to display key projects or featured products from each sector with brief descriptions and imagery.
- Contact & Inquiry Form: An intuitive contact form allowing visitors to inquire about specific services or sectors, facilitating direct communication.
- Responsive Design: Ensure the website provides an optimal viewing and interaction experience across various devices and screen sizes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated deep blue (#244786) to convey professionalism and technological stability. Chosen to reflect the industrial and precise nature of some sectors, while maintaining a clean aesthetic appropriate for a corporate portfolio. This color will be prominent in headers, accents, and important elements.
- Background color: A very light, almost off-white with a hint of blue (#EEF1F6), providing a clean and spacious canvas that harmonizes subtly with the primary color and supports readability in a light color scheme.
- Accent color: A vibrant aqua-green (#38CBEC), which serves as a fresh, dynamic contrast to the primary blue. This color highlights interactive elements, call-to-action buttons, and signifies innovation and growth, particularly for the hydroponic sector.
- Neodymium Sector Palette: A dedicated blue color palette to distinguish the Neodymium Magnets section, emphasizing precision and technological advancement.
- Signages Sector Palette: A distinct purple color palette to highlight the Signages section, conveying creativity and visual impact.
- Hydroponic Farming Sector Palette: A fresh green color palette for the Hydroponic Farming section, reflecting nature, growth, and sustainability.
- Headlines: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly technical, and bold presence, ideal for capturing attention.
- Body Text: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability and a clean, neutral appearance, suitable for extensive content like sector descriptions and 'About Us' details.
- Modern, minimalist line-art icons that are sector-specific (e.g., stylized magnet, abstract signage motif, simplified leaf/water for hydroponics) to enhance visual communication and user navigation.
- A clean, modular grid-based layout ensuring clear separation and easy access to content for each of the three diverse sectors. Emphasize a visually spacious design to present information clearly.
- Subtle, fluid hover animations for interactive elements and smooth transitions when navigating between sections or displaying gallery items, adding a polished and modern feel without distraction.
